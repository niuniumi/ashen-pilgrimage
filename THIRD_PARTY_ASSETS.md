# Third-party asset attribution

## Game-icons.net

Card illustrations and interface icon silhouettes are adapted from the
[Game-icons.net](https://game-icons.net/) collection and distributed under
[CC BY 3.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/).

Per-icon creator names and original vector paths are retained in
`qa/source-art/icon-vectors/game-icons/manifest.json`. The generated pixel
atlases add an original limited-color treatment, material framing, texture,
and low-resolution rasterization for Ashen Pilgrimage.
